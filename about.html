About Electro Tech Projects

Professional Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning Specialists.

Your Trusted HVAC Partner

Electro Tech Projects is a professional Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning (HVAC) company based in Midrand, South Africa. We provide reliable installation, maintenance and repair services for residential, commercial and industrial clients.

Our experienced technicians deliver quality workmanship while ensuring every project meets the highest safety and industry standards.

We pride ourselves on offering energy-efficient HVAC solutions that improve comfort, reduce electricity costs and increase equipment lifespan.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, affordable and energy-efficient HVAC solutions while exceeding customer expectations through excellent workmanship and professional service.

Our Vision

To become one of South Africa's leading HVAC companies recognised for quality, innovation and customer satisfaction.
